Chicken Curry Recipe

5 stars

Ingredients

Amount Food Item
6 chicken legs
6 chicken thighs
1 whole medium yellow onion
1 whole Cinnamon stick
1 Bay Leaf
2 whole Cloves
5 tblspn Extra virgin olive oil
1/2 teaspoon each of minced garlic and ginger
1/2 tomato
2 1/2 teaspoons tumeric
1 teaspoon cumin
2 teaspoon paprika
1 1/2 teaspoon chilli powder
1 teaspoon cardamom
1 teaspoon gram marsala
(Optional) 2 Tblspns Cashew butter and 1 Tblspn Heavy Cream

Steps

  1. Put your olive oil in the pan and sizze oil on high heat.
  2. Add in your cinnamon stick, bayleaf, and cloves to the pot.
  3. Add in the onion once it you smell the cinnamon and bayleaf.
  4. Once onions are soft, add in your tomatos. Stir.
  5. After tomatos are melted, add in your ginger and garlic.
  6. Stir periodically and lower the heat to medium.
  7. After about 5 minutes, add in all your powdered spices, and mix.
  8. The color will change to a brownish orange.
  9. Add in all of the chicken.
  10. Cover the pot, and let cook for 40 minutes. stir frequently.
  11. The water from the chicken will come out and make the curry
  12. (optional)For a thicker curry, increase heat, add 2tblspns of cashew butter and 1 tablespoon of heavy cream. lower heat after 15 minutes.
  13. Let sit for 30 minutes with no heat before serving.
